Cricut.com/Setup



Are you looking for a machine that will cut your designs in appropriate shape then you are at the right place? Yes, here cricut.com/setup, the best machine in today's era is ready to cut designs for you. The materials that the machine can cut include vinyl, cardboard, cardstock, paper, leather, balsa wood, bass, and many more. The Cricut machines come in 3 different yet main types i.e. Cricut maker, Cricut explore, and Cricut joy. All these machines have their unique specialties.

Although the word circuit setup sounds familiar but is quite important for the first-time user to know the actual setup of the Cricut machine. The actual setup of the Cricut machine is given below with the proper guidelines. Please go through it and complete the setup of your Cricut machine.

Cricut.com/setup- The overview:

In this process, you will be able to know the overall steps included in the Cricut setup. Accordingly, the detailed steps are given below.

  • Unbox your Cricut machine and place it on a table.
  • Keep enough space at each end of the Cricut machine.
  • Now open the flap and then turn on the machine.
  • The Cricut machine is available for Windows, MAC, iOS, and Android systems.
  • Go to cricut.com/setup on your Pc or laptop.
  • Download the Cricut driver known as Cricut design space from this site.
  • Then install the design space application.
  • Now, create the Cricut id by providing some specific information.
  • Finally, connect your device using the Bluetooth connection.
  • Here you are now ready to cut the designs using your Cricut machine.

How to download and install the Cricut design space application?

The process to download is similar for windows and MAC, iOS, and Android. Both are explained just below;

Windows/MAC:

  • Open the internet browser on your device.
  • Then search for the cricut.com/setup link.
  • Select the machine as Cricut.
  • Then select the model of your Cricut machine.
  • Click on the 'I agree to Cricut terms of use and privacy policy' checkbox.
  • As soon as you tick the checkbox, the download button becomes visible.
  • Then click on the download button and allow the file to download.
  • Wait for a few seconds till the download completes.
  • Then go to the downloads folder and double click on the file.
  • In windows, press the yes button to allow installation.
  • For MAC click on the continue button and enter the password if required.
  • The installation will take a few seconds.
  • Now your design space application is installed successfully.

iOS/Android:

  • Turn on your android/iOS handset.
  • Go to the play store/app store on the respective devices.
  • Then search the Cricut design space application here.
  • As soon as the application appears, either click on Install or Get on the application interface.
  • Wait for the download and the simultaneous installation to occur.
  • Then finally open the application after the installation to continue the next process.

How to create the Cricut Id?

Cricut Id is as simple as creating an account on the Cricut design space application. The Id helps you to store and access the content on the Cricut design space application. So let's know the steps to create the Cricut id.

  • First of all, open the Cricut.com/setup design space application.
  • Then click on the Product Setup button.
  • On the next tab, the form will appear.
  • Here enter your email address i.e.valid.
  • Then create a strong password for that email address or for your Cricut account.
  • Enter the first name, and last name, and then finally select your country.
  • Tick both the checkbox and then finally click on the create Cricut id button.
  • The Cricut id is now created.
  • Use the email address and password to the login details.

How to connect the Cricut machine to the device using Bluetooth?

The Bluetooth connection is the most advanced connection to connect your Cricut machine to the device. One more option available for the users is the USB connection. The USB connection is just plugging the USB cable at both ends. But the Bluetooth connection differs in all the systems.

Windows/MAC:

  • For Windows and MAC devices, turn on the device.
  • Go to the settings option.
  • Then go to the Bluetooth settings.
  • You can also directly navigate to the shortcut Bluetooth symbol present at the lower bottom i.e. on the taskbar of your device desktop.
  • After entering into the Bluetooth settings, click on the Add a device button.
  • A new window appears.
  • In this window, just select the connection type as Bluetooth.
  • Then the Bluetooth will start searching the nearby connections available.
  • Now the Cricut.com/setup Bluetooth will automatically turn on as soon as you turn on the Cricut machine.
  • Just select the Cricut maker Bluetooth connection and click on the pair button.
  • If necessary simply enter the password as '0000' or'1234'.
  • Finally, the connection is established successfully.

Android/iOS:

  • For the Android/iOS system, just pull the menu bar down.
  • It is also called the shortcut bar to reach the settings.
  • Here there will be the Bluetooth shortcut button present.
  • Just click on the button and hold it till the Bluetooth settings open.
  • Now turn on the Bluetooth and scroll down.
  • Wait for the Cricut Bluetooth becomes visible in the available device list.
  • Now select the Cricut Bluetooth and press the pair button.
  • Enter the password like '0000' or '1234'.
  • Now the Bluetooth connection will establish.

How to cut various designs on the Cricut machine?

To cut various designs on the Cricut machines you will need to take the first cut on the Cricut design space application. After the first cut you just need to;

  • Open the Cricut machine as well as the Cricut design space application.
  • Then go to the insert button.
  • Either insert the design from your local folders or just use the readymade templates.
  • Then insert the proper pointer into the tool holder kit of the Cricut machine.
  • Insert the material into the tray.
  • Now press the cut button present on the Cricut design space application.
  • Load up the material into the Cricut machine.
  • Finally, press the go button present on the Cricut machine.
  • Within a few seconds to a few minutes, the Cricut machine will cut the design for you.

In this way, you can set up the cricut.com/setup on your own.
